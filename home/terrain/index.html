Over the last few years Aj Battle has been creating terrain for friends, clients and clubs.

With a background in Fine art Aj uses a wide variety of techniques for making terrain including; sculpting, mould making, painting and kitbashing.

Settings have included; forests, jungles, snowscapes, deserts, wastelands, ruined cities and medieval towns.
