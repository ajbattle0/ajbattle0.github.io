As part of the feudal world terrain set.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

These pieces were designed to be playable in larger games.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The two tall pieces block line of sight nicely to let armies play defensively.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The medium buildings are made with large flat areas to allow models to be positioned.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The smaller pieces are to decorate the board and allow units to recieve cover in areas they otherwise wouldn’t.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When making these I went for a dark palette of blues and browns to give the terrain the feeling of a dark town on a overcast day.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
